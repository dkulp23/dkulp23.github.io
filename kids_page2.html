Kids in Gardens

It's a known fact that that gardening extends your life.

A great healthy fun way to burn off energy for kids is getting their hands dirty in the garden. So why not spend a few hours in your home garden or the neighborhood pea patch.

Grow a plant from seed.

All you need is a pot or a cup with holes in them for drainage. Some seeds, water, and sunny spot on the window sill. Then watch it grow. Don't forget to water every couple of days. But don't over water. Consider adding a calenar on the refrigerator to mark the days to water.
